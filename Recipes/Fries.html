French Fries

These fries are Chef Johns specialty fries. Once you've succesfully made these all other options for fries will never feel the same

  • One large Russet potato, cut evenly sized strips
  • Vegtable oil for frying, about 2 cups or as needed
  • salt to taste

Steps

  1. Soak potato stips in large bowl of water for about 30 minutes
  2. pat with paper towels until thoroughly dry
  3. Heat oil in a deep-fryer OR large sauce pan to 275 degrees F (135 degrees C). Gently add potatoes to the hot oil and fry for 5 minutes, stirring and flipping the potatoes
  4. Use a slotted spoon to transfer the potatoes to paper towel-lined plate. let cool completely
  5. heat oil again, but this time to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). add potatoes and fry a second time until golden brown, 5 to 6 minutes
  6. Remove from the deep-fryer and blot with paper towel. sprinkle with salt to serve
  7. Enjoy!
