Garlic Chili Noodles

Not made for people with Mild palate! These easy made noodles can be made quick by anyone!

  • 1 package of ramen noodles
  • 2 teaspoons sesame oil
  • 2 teaspoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon chiu chow style chili oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 pinch black sesame seeds
  • 2 green onions, sliced diagonally, or to taste
  1. Bring a pot of water to boil over high heat. add ramen noodles, and cook until tender, 3 to 4 minutes
  2. Meanwhile, heat sesame oil, soy sauce, and chili oil in a wok over medium-high heat
  3. add garlic and stir fry until fragrant, 30 to 45 seconds, remove from heat. Drain ramen noodles, add to the wok, and toss to coat. Sprinkle with sesame seeds and scalions to garnish
  4. ENJOY!
